\subsection{Evaluation stance}

Pilot evaluation assesses \textbf{governance feasibility}, \textbf{operator workflow utility}, and
\textbf{privacy posture}---not validated harm reduction unless separately approved and powered.

\begin{table}[htbp]
  \centering
  \caption{Evaluation metric categories (governed pilots).}
  \label{tab:eval-metrics}
  \small
  \begin{tabular}{@{}p{2.4cm}p{3.8cm}p{4.8cm}@{}}
    \toprule
    Category & Examples & Method \\
    \midrule
    Situational awareness & Comprehension probes & Tabletop + live exercises \\
    Operator workflow & Time-to-review, overrides & Dashboard logs \\
    Risk communication & Explanation clarity & Staff surveys (ethics approved) \\
    Privacy & Retention audits, egress tests & Technical + DPO audit \\
    Protocol alignment & Mapping correctness & Structured debrief \\
    Workload & Queue depth, alert fatigue & Logs + interviews \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
\end{table}

Data minimization: no student-identifiable evaluation corpora; separate operational and research
datasets; retention approved by DPO.
Horizon partners may harmonize metric definitions and anonymized cross-site templates.
Full metric definitions: companion research publications and repository documentation.
