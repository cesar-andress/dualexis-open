\section{How to Use This Document Pack}
\label{sec:how-to-use}

\textbf{Executive Brief} (separate PDF, \texttt{executive\_brief.pdf}): 6--8 pages for school leadership,
ethics committees, and non-technical stakeholders---purpose, boundaries, pilot pathway, and
governance gates.

\textbf{This document} (requirements body + appendices): deployment rules, privacy and DPIA checklist,
operational SOPs, pilot programme with go/no-go and stop conditions, evaluation and risk summary,
governance templates, budgets, \textbf{Appendix~E hardware BOM}, and technical reference for IT teams.

Both documents share version \docversion\ (\docid).

\textbf{Suggested first steps:} (1)~circulate the Executive Brief to leadership and DPO;
(2)~hold a steering workshop on Box~\ref{box:system-boundaries} and pause/stop rights;
(3)~schedule a protocol-mapping workshop before any live sensing;
(4)~use Section~\ref{sec:circulation-guide} for audience-specific routing and community communication.
