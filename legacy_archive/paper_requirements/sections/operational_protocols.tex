\section{Operational Protocols and Staff Workflows}
\label{sec:operational-protocols}

\subsection{Integration with school emergency protocols}

DUALEXIS sits \textbf{upstream} of institutional decisions:
\begin{enumerate}[noitemsep]
  \item approved sensors produce zone-level semantic events under privacy policy;
  \item site coordination fuses context and drafts \textbf{advisory} recommendations;
  \item safety staff review the queue and record disposition;
  \item staff activate existing protocols (PA, radio, municipal liaison) per the school SMS.
\end{enumerate}

The system does not replace PA, emergency services, or union agreements.

\subsection{Tabletop exercises (mandatory before live sensing)}

Conduct \textbf{at least two} tabletop exercises per MVP site before live occupant sensing:
\begin{enumerate}[noitemsep]
  \item exit impairment / congestion scenario;
  \item multimodal conflict or evacuation-recommendation scenario.
\end{enumerate}

Use synthetic or sandboxed feeds; debrief comprehension, protocol mapping, alert policy, and
workload.
Document remediations before go/no-go (Section~\ref{sec:pilot-programme}).

\subsection{Escalation and governance cadence}

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item \textbf{On-site safety lead} --- first review authority;
  \item \textbf{Administrator} --- high/critical advisory escalation;
  \item \textbf{IT duty officer} --- infrastructure faults;
  \item \textbf{DPO} --- privacy incidents and data-subject requests;
  \item \textbf{Research lead (UCJC)} --- system anomalies, not operational dispatch.
\end{itemize}

Weekly steering meetings; monthly governance review; end-of-phase report for each tier.
