\subsection{Target environments and stakeholders}

Pilots target \textbf{confined educational spaces}: primary/secondary schools, multi-building
campuses, indoor zones (hallways, stairwells, cafeterias, exits).
Outdoor perimeters are out of scope unless explicitly approved with governance review.

\subsection{Pilot objectives (planning level)}

\textbf{Primary:} evaluate whether privacy-preserving zone summaries support safety staff
decision support and SMS integration under ethics-approved conditions.

\textbf{Success at this stage:} governance charter, protocol mapping, DPIA in progress, training
plan, ethics approval where required---\textbf{not} proof that technology prevents harm.
