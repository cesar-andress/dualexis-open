\section{Governance Templates and Workshop Aids}
\label{app:governance-templates}

Templates are \textbf{planning aids}.
Legal counsel and the institution's DPO must adapt them to jurisdiction and school policy.

\subsection{Pilot governance charter (outline)}

\begin{enumerate}[noitemsep]
  \item \textbf{Parties} --- school legal entity; research partner (if any); steering committee members.
  \item \textbf{Purpose} --- evaluate governed decision support for emergency-plan alignment only.
  \item \textbf{Scope} --- approved zone list; modalities; staffed hours; phase end date; co-design record.
  \item \textbf{Boundaries} --- incorporate Box~\ref{box:system-boundaries} by reference.
  \item \textbf{Roles} --- leadership, safety lead, IT, DPO, research (advisory only).
  \item \textbf{Decision rights} --- go/no-go, stop conditions, scope changes, pause per zone.
  \item \textbf{Community communication} --- transparency notice version; consultation log.
  \item \textbf{Reporting} --- weekly steering; monthly governance summary.
  \item \textbf{Signatures} --- leadership, DPO, safety lead (minimum).
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Plain-language transparency notice (outline)}

For parents, staff, and (where appropriate) students:

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item \textbf{What is in scope:} named zones (hallways, cafeterias, exits)---monitoring \emph{spaces},
        not individuals by name.
  \item \textbf{What we do not do:} facial recognition, biometrics, disciplinary automation,
        automatic lockdown, cloud video storage.
  \item \textbf{Who sees summaries:} trained safety staff and authorized administrators only.
  \item \textbf{How long records are kept:} as stated in the school DPIA (structured summaries and audit logs).
  \item \textbf{Your voice:} how to contact the DPO; how to request pause, exclusion, or steering review.
  \item \textbf{Version and date:} (insert); previous versions archived.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Parent / staff information sheet (short form)}

One-page Q\&A suitable for backpacks or staff rooms:
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item Is this facial recognition? \textbf{No.}
  \item Will the school lock down automatically? \textbf{No}---staff decide all steps.
  \item Are video recordings kept? \textbf{Not by default}---see DPIA for structured logs only.
  \item Can we stop? \textbf{Yes}---pause, shrink scope, or halt under stop conditions.
  \item Who oversees privacy? \textbf{School DPO} and steering committee.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Protocol-mapping workshop (half-day agenda)}

\textbf{Participants:} safety lead, administrator, facilities representative, DPO (or delegate),
steering chair; research observer only if invited.

\begin{enumerate}[noitemsep]
  \item Review Box~1 boundaries and existing SMS / emergency plan (30 min).
  \item Walk floor plan; agree co-designed zone list and excluded sensitive areas (45 min).
  \item Map each summary type to SMS branch, reviewer role, and escalation path (60 min).
  \item Agree staffed-hours review policy, substitutes, and false-alarm handling (30 min).
  \item Draft communication timeline (Section~\ref{tab:phased-comms}) (20 min).
  \item Sign or schedule follow-up for protocol matrix (Appendix table below).
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Tabletop exercise record (outline)}

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item date, participants, roles played, scenario description (synthetic/sandbox only);
  \item comprehension and protocol-mapping findings;
  \item handoff and advisory-boundary issues;
  \item false-alarm / review-queue adjustments;
  \item remediations before go/no-go;
  \item signatures: safety lead, steering representative.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Phased communication checklist (internal)}

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item Phase~0: steering only; no public ``camera installation'' messaging.
  \item Phase~1: staff aware of sandbox/tabletop; parents if required by DPO/ethics.
  \item Phase~2: published transparency notice; signage; DPO contact live.
  \item After any stop condition: internal incident review before external statements.
  \item Horizon / research: no school-identifiable quotes without written authorization.
\end{itemize}
